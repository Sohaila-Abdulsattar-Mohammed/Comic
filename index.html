D2 DOOR DISASTER

This is you, an NYUAD student— and you're hungry. In search of a “good” meal, you go to the legendary Dining Hall 2, known affectionately as D2. In the mood for a meal, you decide to head there.

You finally reach D2 after an arduous, long hike. Finally, upon the doors of the grand institution, you pull on the door— surprisingly to no avail. You pull harder— still the doors don’t budge.

You try and try again, the smell of delicious food so tantalizingly close yet still out of reach. Out of frustration, you kick the door— hard— just hoping the doors would open already. Little did you know that that was going to be a mistake.

You turn around to look back at the door, to your sheer horror, an angered face has appeared on the D2 door. Out of sheer shock, you stumble back and fall. The door has become ALIVE!!!

The door grows arms and it's ready to fight! You prepare yourself to face off against the door.

You triumph, and finally, you enter D2, earning your well-deserved breakfast
