The NYUAD Hunger Quest

Meet Josh, an NYUAD student and survivor of the four-hour starvation block built into his class schedule.

His destination: the legendary East Dining Hall, or as the locals call it... D2.

The mission is clear. The path... long. The appetite... unstoppable.

After an exhausting trek across campus, Josh finally arrives at D2. But as he grab the door handles and pulls... nothing. He tries again... still nothing. The doors of D2 stand unmoved, mocking his hunger.

Josh's determination turns to desperation. He pulls, he yanks, he summons every bit of gym strength he swore he'd been building. In a final act of desperation, he kicks the door... a terrible choice.

The air falls silent. A faint vibration hums through the frame. Then, two glowing eyes appear on the glass, glaring back at him. The D2 door has awakened! And it is furious.

Metal groans. Hinges twist. Arms emerge from the doorway like steel vines. The once-innocent entrance now stands ready for battle. Josh clenches his fists, he'll do whatever it takes to get inside.

The door crashes open, defeated at last! Josh, breathless but victorious, claims his hard-earned breakfast, the taste of triumph sweet on his tongue!
